# What CGPA Do You Need to Graduate from Pakistani Universities? (2026)

Failing a course or having a rough semester raises one scary question: will my CGPA be too low to graduate? Here are the official minimum requirements for all major Pakistani universities.

## Minimum CGPA for Bachelor's Degree

| University | Minimum CGPA | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| NUST | 2.0 / 4.0 | Below 2.0 triggers probation first |
| FAST-NUCES | 2.0 / 4.0 | Must also clear all core courses |
| UET Lahore | 2.0 / 4.0 | Engineering programs only |
| COMSATS (CUI) | 2.0 / 4.0 | Standard HEC minimum |
| Punjab University | 2.0 / 4.0 | Per HEC guidelines |
| PIEAS | 2.0 / 4.0 | Strictly enforced |
| GIKI | 2.0 / 4.0 | Plus no outstanding F grades |
| Bahria University | 2.0 / 4.0 | Standard HEC |
| Karachi University | 2.0 / 4.0 | Per HEC |
| Air University | 2.0 / 4.0 | HEC standard |

## Minimum CGPA for Master's Programs (MS/MPhil)

Graduate programs have **higher minimum requirements**:

| University | MS/MPhil Minimum CGPA |
|---|---|
| NUST | **3.0 / 4.0** |
| PIEAS | **3.0 / 4.0** |
| FAST-NUCES | **2.5 / 4.0** |
| Most HEC universities | **2.5 / 4.0** |

## HEC Policy on Graduation Requirements

The Higher Education Commission (HEC) mandates that **no Pakistani university can award a Bachelor's degree to a student with CGPA below 2.0**. This is a national minimum floor — individual universities can be stricter.

## What Happens If Your CGPA Falls Below 2.0?

1. **First Semester Below 2.0**: Academic Warning issued.
2. **Second Consecutive Semester Below 2.0**: Academic Probation.
3. **Third Consecutive Semester Below 2.0**: Most universities initiate discontinuation proceedings.

Students on probation are often restricted from: extracurricular activities, society memberships, and (at some universities) internship programs.

## How to Recover a Low CGPA

The most effective strategies:

1. **Retake failed or low-grade courses.** At most Pakistani universities, repeated courses replace the original grade in the CGPA calculation.
2. **Take elective courses strategically.** Lighter courses (Islamiyat, Pakistan Studies, Humanities) can give you easy A grades to boost the weighted average.
3. **Target all sessional marks.** Quizzes and assignments are often underrated — students who ace sessional marks routinely outperform others in final standing.

## When Does GPA Matter Beyond Graduation?

- **Jobs**: Many MNCs, banks, and government bodies require CGPA ≥ 2.5 or 3.0 in their eligibility criteria.
- **MS Admission**: Most Pakistani and foreign universities require 3.0+ for their graduate programs.
- **HEC Scholarships**: HEC Indigenous Scholarship requires CGPA ≥ 2.5.
- **PM Laptop Scheme**: Typically requires a defined minimum CGPA set each year.
