The **Prime Minister’s Laptop Scheme** is one of the most anticipated government initiatives for students in Pakistan. Designed to bridge the digital divide and empower students with the tools needed for research and modern education, the 2026 cycle is expected to be bigger than ever.

In this guide, we break down the latest updates for the 2026-2027 phase, how to check your eligibility, and the step-by-step process to ensure you don't miss out.

---

## What is the PM Laptop Scheme 2026?
The scheme, executed by the [Higher Education Commission (HEC)](https://www.hec.gov.pk) of Pakistan, distributes high-specification laptops to merit-based students across public sector universities. The goal is to facilitate students in their academic pursuits, freelancing, and research work.

## Eligibility Criteria (Expected for 2026)
To be eligible for a free laptop under the PM scheme, students must meet the following criteria:

### 1. Enrollment Status
- Must be a student of a **Public Sector University** or Degree Awarding Institution (DAI) recognized by the HEC.
- Students of private universities are currently **not eligible** for this specific scheme.
- Check the [List of Eligible Universities](/deadlines) (Public Sector).

### 2. Academic Level
- **Ph.D./MS/M.Phil.** students.
- **Masters** (16-year education) students.
- **Bachelors** (4-year or 5-year) program students.
- Students enrolled in Government Degree Colleges (for certain regions like ICT).

### 3. Merit Requirements
- For Semester System: A minimum CGPA of **3.0 or higher** (varies by university quota).
- For Annual System: Minimum **70% marks** in the previous annual examination.
- **Tip:** Ensure your GPA is accurate. Use our [GPA Calculator](/tools/gpa-calculator) to double-check your latest results.

## How to Apply Online: Step-by-Step
Applying is easy, but you must be precise with your data. Follow these steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to the [HEC PM Laptop Portal](https://laptop.hec.gov.pk/).
2.  **Registration:** Click on "Apply Now" and enter your CNIC/B-Form number.
3.  **Profile Setup:** Fill in your personal details, including your active email and mobile number.
4.  **Academic Details:** Select your university, department, and current semester. Ensure your CGPA matches your latest transcript exactly.
5.  **Submission:** Review your application and submit. You will receive a confirmation email.

## The Merit Calculation (How Laptops are Awarded)
The HEC uses a transparent "Merit Formula" to distribute a limited number of laptops.
- **Quota System:** Laptops are distributed based on university enrollment size.
- **Departmental Ranking:** You compete against students within your own department/campus.
- **CGPA Weightage:** Higher CGPAs have the highest priority. If two students have the same CGPA, the HEC looks at their previous academic records (FSc/Matric).

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Can I apply if I already received a laptop in a previous phase?
**No.** If you have already received a laptop under any government scheme (PM Scheme, CM Punjab Scheme, etc.), you are disqualified.

### Q2: Is there a fee for registration?
**No.** The PM Laptop Scheme registration is **100% Free**.

### Q3: Which laptop models are being distributed in 2026?
While the exact model varies, the 2026 phase is expected to include laptops with at least **8GB RAM**, **SSD storage**, and **Core i5 (12th Gen or higher)**.
